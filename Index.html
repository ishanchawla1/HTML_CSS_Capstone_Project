"Innovation in the Open"

GOOGLE I/O

2020

I/O brings together developers from around the globe for talks, hands-on learning with Google experts, and a first look at Google’s latest developer products.

2020.07.07(TUE) ~ 10(THU)

@ Shoreline Amphitheatre in Mountain View, California.

Main Program

Keynote

Learn about the latest product and platform innovations at Google in a Keynote led by Sundar Pichai.

Codelabs

Get hands-on with the latest and greatest Google technologies by trying one of over 100 brand-new and updated Codelabs.

Sandboxes

Join us where we demo things that are new this year. Developers can directly interact with the Googlers who work on these things and ask questions.

Sessions

Join us for demo of things that are new this year. Developers can directly interact with the Googlers who work on these things and ask questions.

Concert

Join us for an exciting night of music starting with Phantogram at 7:30 PM PDT, followed by the main act Justice from 8:30-10 PM.

Featured Guests

Sundar Pichai
Chief Executive Officer of Alphabet
Pichai Sundararajan, also known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian American business executive, the chief executive officer of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Pichai began his career as a materials engineer and joined Google as a management executive in 2004.
Nikesh Arora
Chief Executive Officer of Palo Alto Networks
Nikesh Arora is an is an Indian American business executive. He is the chief executive officer and chairman of Palo Alto Networks. Arora was formerly a senior executive at Google. He served as the president of SoftBank Group from October 2014 to June 2016.
James Tamplin
Board Member at Charm Industrial
James Tamplin. James Tamplin. I'm currently on sabbatical. Previously, I co-founded and led Firebase from inception through acquisition, and on to become Google's flagship app development platform.
Susan Wojcicki
CEO of YouTube
Susan Diane Wojcicki is an American technology executive. She has been the CEO of YouTube since February 2014. Wojcicki was involved in the founding of Google, and became Google's first marketing manager in 1999.
Elon Musk
CEO of SpaceX
Elon Reeve Musk FRS is an engineer, industrial designer, technology entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is a citizen of South Africa, the United States, and Canada.
Oprah Winfrey
American executive
Oprah Gail Winfrey is an American media executive, actress, talk show host, television producer, and philanthropist.
Ruchi Sanghvi
Engineer
Ruchi Sanghvi is an Indian computer engineer. She was the first female engineer hired by Facebook. In late 2010, she quit Facebook and in 2011, she started her own company Cove, with two other co-founders. The company was sold to Dropbox in 2012 and Sanghvi joined Dropbox as VP of Operations.
Sheryl Sandberg
Chief Operating Officer of Facebook
Sheryl Kara Sandberg is an American billionaire technology executive, author. She is the chief operating officer of Facebook, and the founder of LeanIn.Org. In June 2012, she was elected to Facebook's board of directors, becoming the first woman to serve on its board.
